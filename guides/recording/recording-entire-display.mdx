---
title: "Recording an entire display"
description: "Capture a full monitor by selecting the Display recording option."
icon: "monitor"
---

To record a full screen:

1. Open the recording picker.
2. Select **Display**.
3. Move your cursor to the display you want to capture.
4. Click to confirm.
