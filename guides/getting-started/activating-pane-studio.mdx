---
title: "Activating Pane Studio"
description: "Activate your Pane Studio license with the email address linked to your account."
icon: "key-round"
---

To activate Pane Studio:

1. Open the app.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Click **Activate License**.

Pane Studio opens the license activation window.

![License activation](/images/guide/002.png)

Enter the email address associated with your account.

Pane Studio sends an activation code to that address. Open your inbox, copy the code, and follow the prompts to finish activation.
